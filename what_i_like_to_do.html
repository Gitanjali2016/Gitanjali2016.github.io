My Hobbies!!

I am a professionally trained Bharatnatyam dancer and obtained my Junior Diploma with First Class.

I am very fond of painting too. Painting has been my hobby ever since I learned how to hold a brush. I never took any special art lessons,
I simply use my imagination and find my inspiration in various objects and creatures around me. Recently,
my paintings were displayed at the Inspire Students Exhibition in the 7th Nobel Laureate Science Conclave, 2014.
